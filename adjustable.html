I am a sophomore student at Case Western Reserve University from Brookline, MA. I am planning on majoring in Data Science and minoring in both Astronomy and Economics. My main interests lie in astrophysics and sports analytics, and I have worked on projects in each of these fields. I am particularly interested in theoretical physics and predictive sports modeling.

Most of the work I do is in Python. The packages that I mostly commonly use are: Matplotlib, NumPy, pandas, and seaborn. I have also used Cartopy, OpenCV, PIL, and TensorFlow for various projects. Other than Python, I have some knowledge in Java, HTML/CSS (plus modest experience with JavaScript and jQuery), and I have briefly used R.

If you would like more information, you can contact me @jms625@case.edu to request a resume or a transcript.
